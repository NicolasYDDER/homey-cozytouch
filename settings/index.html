About Protocols

Atlantic uses two cloud backends depending on the device generation:

CozyTouch (Magellan) — For newer devices: recent boilers, towel racks (Kelud), AC units (Takao).
Overkiz — For older devices: water heaters (Calypso, Zeneo), Thermor and Sauter branded products.

The app automatically queries both backends when you pair devices. A single set of credentials works for both.
